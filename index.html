Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter (or oil)
  • Filling (optional): cheese, diced veggies, ham, fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium head and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add the filling (optional): Once the eggs begin to set at the edge but still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your favorite fillings to one side of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto the plate.
  6. Enjoy! Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if you wish.
